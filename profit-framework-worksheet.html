Evaluate Your Micro-SaaS Tool Ideas

P - Problem Severity (1-10)

How painful is the problem? 8-10 = People pay immediately | 5-7 = Need convincing | 1-4 = Skip it

R - Reach (Market Size)

1M+ = 10 points | 500K-1M = 8 points | 100K-500K = 6 points | <100K = 3 points

O - Overhead (Complexity)

Simple (1-2 features) = 10 points | Medium (3-5) = 6 points | Complex = 3 points

F - Frequency (Usage Pattern)

Daily = 10 points | Weekly = 7 points | Monthly = 5 points | Rarely = 2 points

I - Income Potential

B2B ($29-299) = 10 points | Prosumer ($9-49) = 7 points | Consumer ($2-19) = 5 points

T - Time to Market

1 week = 10 points | 2-4 weeks = 6 points | 1+ months = 3 points
