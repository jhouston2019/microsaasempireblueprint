## üé• Video Production Tips

### Equipment Needed
- **Camera**: iPhone/Android or webcam
- **Microphone**: USB microphone or headset
- **Lighting**: Ring light or natural light
- **Software**: Loom, OBS, or screen recording tool

### Recording Setup
- **Screen recording**: For tool demonstrations
- **Talking head**: For personal connection
- **Slides**: For key concepts and frameworks
- **Examples**: Real-world applications

### Video Structure
1. **Hook** (30 seconds): Grab attention
2. **Introduction** (1 minute): What you'll learn
3. **Main content** (8-12 minutes): Core lesson
4. **Action steps** (1-2 minutes): What to do next
5. **CTA** (30 seconds): Next steps
